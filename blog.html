SIGNIFICANCE OF ENGLISH LANGUAGE

  • Kenme
  • Dec 1, 2020

“To learn a language is to have one more window from which to look at the world”. This saying is absolutely true about English language. Known as the ‘window to the world’, it opens up a lot of possibilities and developments for an individual. The importance of English is really prominent all over the world. This importance is traced back to the British Empire, which at its peak covered 25% of earth’s surface. During the colonial times, the British often compelled people in those countries to speak English rather than their native language. Even if the origin of English language is complicated, it has left an important mark on media, trade and business.

A lot of areas proclaim the importance of English language. One of the most prominent is how it opens up the possibilities for an individual. It gives you wings to fly all over the world and explore different things. As English is rightly called the global language, it is said that one out of five people can speak or at least understand the language. English is the official language of 53 countries and is used as lingua franca by people from all around the world. This means if you are working in Africa or travelling to Japan, studying English can help you have a conversation with people from all over the world.

English is the predominant language in all fields of life. If you are someone who is enthusiastic about scientific advancements you will find yourself in need of learning English. In the last century, the number of scientific papers written in English has started to outweigh the number of papers written in the native language of the researcher. For this reason, having a knowledge of English is incredibly important to those working in the scientific fields and science enthusiasts.

English is also the most prevalent language of the media industry because of the dominance of Hollywood in global media, an enormous number of films, TV shows and popular songs are written in English. So, if you learn and speak English, you won’t need to rely on translations and subtitles anymore to enjoy your favourite books, songs, films and TV shows.

When it comes to the most convenient technology ever found, internet, also projects English as an important language. More than half the content on the internet is written in English. English is one of the most important languages for business. It is considered so, due to being the de facto language of The United States, and the official language of the UK, Canada, India and South Africa.

Finally, learning English gives you access to multiple cultures and provide with a first-hand experience. Good knowledge of English will allow you to access films, musics and literature from hundreds of countries around the globe. You can enjoy a lot of books from across the world that are translated into English. Few experiences will make you grow as a person more than learning the values, habits and way of life in a culture that is different from yours.

HOW ENGLISH HELPS TO MOVE UP IN THE WORLD

  • Kenme
  • Dec 10, 2020

There are many reasons why learning English is a wonderful idea. The horizons one can reach by learning to speak English is countless. Learning English can open up the door of lot of career opportunists. It could change your entire future by opening new career possibilities weren’t available before.

If you speak only your native language, your job opportunities may be more limited. When you speak English, you will be able to look for jobs outside of your home country, which increases the area of your job market dramatically. Also, if you like living in another country, having a job offer to get you there is a big plus.

If you have English speaking skills, it will help you broaden your job search within your own country. Companies that have headquarters in English speaking countries frequently set up additional offices in other countries. Depending on the nature of one’s work, the ability to speak English may be a main requirement.

Along with this, knowing a second language will give you an advantage over an equally skilled candidate who speaks only one language. Learning a foreign language demonstrates you are dedicated to self-improvement- a valued asset in any employee.

If you are a working person, learning English will help in achieving more opportunities and you will be eligible for promotion because of your language skills. For example, if your company needs employees to travel to an English-speaking country or to interact with colleagues or partners who are more comfortable speaking English, you may be bestowed with an opportunity to handle these responsibilities thanks to your English language skills.

Your English-speaking skills will also help in building a cordial relationship with your colleagues if you work in a multi-national company. It will also allow you to make new connections and friendships.

Having all these helpful traits will help you in a long run, in addition to making you a more valuable employee. Therefore, by learning a new language, you are truly investing in your future and your ability to provide for yourself and your family.

WHY ENGLISH MAKES KERALITIES SCARED STIFF?

  • Kenme
  • Jan 13, 2020

Even after sporting the highest literacy rate, Keralites still face a lot of difficulty when it comes to speaking the Queen’s English. The poor English communication skills among a huge majority of Keralites validates to this fact. Even those who are highly educated try to disentangle from the situations when they are required to speak in English. The answer to why is it so is a complicated one.

The major problem behind the difficulty is not having a right place to learn. Even though English is a subject in all the government schools, the way it is being taught is really complicated starting from grammar, many schools struggle to provide a better standard of learning. Most of the time, English is not being taught by an English teacher, but teachers of other subjects. This is adversely affecting the quality of Keralites’ English language.

Another worrying aspect is the thick accent that Keralites are struggling with even though having an accent is not exclusive to Malayali alone, it is a real bummer when they are asked to speak especially in front of people who handle the language in a good way.

Bad communication skill is another factor that is troubling a huge number of Keralites, as lack of confidence and inability to pronounce words badly affect the language speakers of Kerala and they end up dreading the language as well as the situations where there is a need of communicating in English.

Despite the preference for English as the medium of instruction in most of the schools, its communicative use is more or restricted to the public domain. Even if a person uses English to communicate, they will be interpreted as arrogant and “show-offish”. As this attitude is dominant in many Malayalees, those who love to speak English also restrict themselves, thereby losing the skills that they have acquired as a result of not having a good platform to speak.

In conclusion, all these problems point towards Keralite’s scarcity in not having a good stage to learn, practice and converse in English. All the above-mentioned troubles can be cleared by having a rostrum, when they are not going to be judged and also be provided with the much-needed confidence. Thus, English won’t be a rocket science anymore for Keralites.

End

ONLINE LEARNING AND ITS GAIN IN POPULARITY

  • Kenme
  • Jan 16, 2020

Online learning has gained a huge popularity during these times. As now, one can split the world into two, pre-COVID and post-COVID. Online learning stands out as such a helpful technology among the masses. Online learning has made learning or gaining knowledge so convenient that there is no looking back and the popularity of the mode of learning is developing day by day. Online learning has to be the greatest revolution in contemporary education. It made a huge change in the system and opened great opportunities for everyone who wants to learn something.

Online learning opens the door to a lot of new possibilities and domains of knowledge. You can learn whatever you want and all it takes is a google search for such online courses. It will also help to unravel the curious learner in you and provide you with a number of ways through which you can explore the different domains of learning. It doesn’t matter where you live and what you want to study, you can always find a suitable course, even a degree program that you can follow from home.

The comfort online classes offer are unparalleled. You can forget about attending classes for hours, sitting in an uncomfortable chair and suffering whole day. there won’t be any physical boundaries, all materials are provided via online platforms, so the accessibility is trouble free, from the comfort of your homes.

An online course looks great on your resume. It will help to convince your potential employer that you are committed to learning and you are eager to obtain more knowledge and new skills. Having a lot of qualifications, be it traditional or not, will really attract your employers and help you in climbing the ladder of your career.

Self-paced learning is another attractive point in online learning. The students can start completing the target at any time, and he/she can arrange a learning schedule that meets his/her individual needs. One major advantage of online learning over traditional college programmes is you will get the time to pursue other hobbies and can do work that you enjoy doing. You can do different things at the same time without giving up on anything.

Lower costs are another striking feature of online learning. Traditional learning takes a lot of expenses including tuition fees, travel and accommodation expenditure and so on. But when it comes to online learning, the expenditure is much less and you don’t have to worry about other expenses related learning new things.

Overall, online learning and degree programs are more convenient and cheaper that their counterparts in traditional education. The best thing about online learning is that you can learn in a relaxed manner. You only need passion for learning and a quick online search that will take you to the right place and right course. From that point on, you will be the master of your own education.
